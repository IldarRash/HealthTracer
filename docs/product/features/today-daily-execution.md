# Today Daily Execution

**Status:** Proposed — current Today supports workouts, checklist completion, adherence, history, and daily reflection; stress, nutrition-today, mental wellbeing checkpoints, and durable habits are incomplete.

## Summary

Today is the user's daily action surface. It should show what the user needs to do today, not every plan or analytics view in the product. The screen combines the current workout, today's nutrition plan, stress or recovery check-in, mental wellbeing checkpoints, habits, checklist completion, and short feedback.

## UX Placement

Today is one of four primary tabs: Chat, Today, Longevity, and Profile. It is the execution surface. Full Training and Nutrition plans are secondary read-only routes opened from Today, Longevity, Chat proposal cards, or Profile context.

## Problem

The product has a daily checklist and workout execution flow, but the user's day is split across separate domains. Today does not yet show the nutrition plan for the day, a first-class stress/wellbeing check-in, or mental health checkpoints. Users should not have to open multiple tabs to know what they need to do today.

## In Scope

- Current workout or movement task for the selected date.
- Today's nutrition plan: meals or meal targets, hydration focus, and adherence entry points.
- Stress, recovery, or wellbeing check-in for the selected date.
- Mental wellbeing checkpoints for the selected date.
- Habit-sourced checklist items from the active habit plan.
- Existing Today checklist items, completion actions, adherence summary, date selection, and short daily reflection.
- Links to full Training and Nutrition weekly views.
- Loading, empty, partial, error, and completed states.

## Out of Scope

- Manual editing of workout or nutrition plans.
- A full weekly training planner on Today.
- A full nutrition planning editor on Today.
- Clinical mental health screening, diagnosis, treatment, or crisis handling generated by AI.
- Vendor readiness scores, biological age, or medical scoring.

## Product Rules

- Today reads from structured state.
- Today completion actions are execution events, not plan edits.
- Workout and nutrition plan changes must happen through Chat proposals and revision-safe backend application.
- Stress, mood, recovery, and mental wellbeing copy must stay wellness-oriented.
- If Today lacks enough data for a card, show a clear empty state and link to Chat or Profile as appropriate.

## User Stories

- As a user, I can open Today and immediately know what workout or movement task is planned.
- As a user, I can see today's nutrition plan and hydration focus without opening a separate primary tab.
- As a user, I can log today's stress or wellbeing state in a quick, non-clinical way.
- As a user, I can complete mental wellbeing checkpoints and habits for today.
- As a user, I can open the full Training or Nutrition plan when I need more detail.
- As a user, I can ask Chat to change a plan instead of editing it manually.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Today renders a current workout card when a workout is scheduled.
- Today renders a nutrition-today card when an active nutrition plan exists.
- Today renders a stress/wellbeing check-in card and stores the result as structured state.
- Today renders mental wellbeing checkpoints and habit checklist items when available.
- Today supports checklist completion and short daily reflection.
- Today links to read-only weekly Training and Nutrition views.
- Today does not expose manual workout-plan or nutrition-plan editing.
- User-visible copy avoids diagnosis, treatment, clinical scoring, or medical certainty.

## Data And API Implications

- Continue using `DailyChecklist` as the daily execution aggregate.
- Add or integrate wellbeing check-in state for mood and stress.
- Add nutrition-today read model derived from the active nutrition plan and adherence.
- Add habit-source metadata when durable habits are implemented.
- Preserve workout session linkage for scheduled workouts.

## AI And Proposal Implications

- Chat can create or adapt Today checklist items through typed proposals.
- Chat can propose workout, nutrition, habit, or recovery changes, but accepted proposals must update structured state before Today reflects them.
- Today can provide static links or prompts to Chat when the user wants a plan change.

## Implementation Slices

1. Current-state alignment: keep workout, checklist, adherence, history, and daily reflection stable.
2. Nutrition-today card: derive meals, hydration, and adherence from active nutrition plan.
3. Wellbeing check-in: mood/stress structured entry and summary.
4. Mental checkpoints: lightweight daily checklist items tied to wellbeing/habit state.
5. Read-only plan links: link to Training and Nutrition weekly views.
6. Copy and safety pass: wellness-only language and empty states.

## Relationship To Other Features

- Mental wellbeing check-ins provide stress and mood capture for Today.
- Habit system materializes durable habits into Today.
- Recovery/readiness provides daily recovery focus without clinical score framing.
- Weekly review and Longevity aggregate Today history; they do not replace Today.
